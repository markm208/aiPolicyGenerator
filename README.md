## AI Policy Generator
This is a tool to specify your AI policy on an assignment. You can select an AI access level and then pick and choose acceptable and unacceptable uses. Once you have specified a policy you can copy it to your clipboard or generate a link to it to share with students.

Access the site here: [ai-policy-generator.html](https://markm208.github.io/aiPolicyGenerator/ai-policy-generator.html)
